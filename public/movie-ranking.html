The Best Movies According to Lara

My top 3 movies of all time

Black Swan

Black Swan is a psychological thriller that delves into the dark side of ambition and the pursuit of perfection. The film's stunning visuals and haunting score create an immersive experience that keeps you on the edge of your seat.

Perfect Blue

Perfect Blue is a psychological thriller that explores the blurred lines between reality and illusion. The film's intense atmosphere and complex narrative make it a captivating watch that lingers long after the credits roll.

Pearl

Pearl is a horror film that delves into the psyche of its titular character, exploring themes of isolation and obsession. The film's chilling atmosphere and unsettling visuals create a haunting experience that stays with you.
